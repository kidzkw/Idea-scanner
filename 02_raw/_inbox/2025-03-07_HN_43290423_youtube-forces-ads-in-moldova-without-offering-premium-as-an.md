# YouTube Forces Ads in Moldova Without Offering Premium as an Option

> **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=43290423

## 原始内容

Until March 2024, users in Moldova enjoyed an ad-free experience on YouTube. However, in March 2024, YouTube began showing intrusive ads in the region. Despite this change, YouTube Premium — the service that allows users to remove ads — remains completely unavailable in Moldova. (https:&#x2F;&#x2F;support.google.com&#x2F;youtube&#x2F;answer&#x2F;6307365)
This situation is frustrating because:
- workarounds don’t work: Using a VPN or changing the country in the YouTube profile doesn’t solve the issue, as payment methods are tied to the user’s actual location.
- no legal alternative exists: Moldovan users cannot subscribe to YouTube Premium to remove ads, unlike users in most other countries.<p>In response, a petition has been started on Change.org urging Google to make YouTube Premium available in Moldova. (https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.change.org&#x2F;p&#x2F;bring-youtube-premium-to-moldova-stop-forced-ads-without-an-option-to-remove-them)<p>Many tried reaching out to support.google, but they only provide formal, generic replies and don’t address the core issue. I’m turning to the HN community for advice: Does anyone know a specific person, team, or organization at YouTube (or Alphabet) who could help with this issue?<p>Curious to hear your thoughts and suggestions.
