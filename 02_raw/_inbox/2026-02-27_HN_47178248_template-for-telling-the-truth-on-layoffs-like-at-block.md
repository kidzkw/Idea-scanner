# Template for telling the truth on layoffs like at Block

> **Source**: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47178248

## 原始内容

For the people who have been laid off, I think the letter by Jack is a disservice. So I crafted a more direct letter. Hope future letters follow this approach.<p>Today we are making one of the hardest decisions in the history of our company: we are reducing our organization by nearly half, from over 10,000 people to just under 6,000. This means over 4,000 of you will be leaving the company.<p>I want to be incredibly direct with you about why this is happening.<p>The Reality of the Decision<p>First, let’s be clear: you are not being replaced by AI. The technology is not better than you, and it didn&#x27;t &quot;outwork&quot; you. The truth is simpler and more frustrating: we planned a specific strategic direction, and it didn&#x27;t work out. We over-extended, and we now need to course-correct to keep the business viable.<p>However, we operate in a market that demands a specific kind of &quot;success&quot; story. If we simply announced that we missed our targets and need to cut costs, the market would react with volatility, punishing our valuation and jeopardizing our remaining operations.<p>The &quot;AI-Enabled&quot; Narrative<p>To protect the company’s standing, we are framing this transition as an evolution into an AI-enabled organization. By attributing these cuts to the &quot;unprecedented efficiency&quot; of intelligence tools and flatter team structures, we signal to the market that we are cutting losses not out of failure, but out of a futuristic lean toward profitability.<p>This is a strategic &quot;win-win&quot; for the company’s narrative and its shareholders. It allows us to cut costs while being perceived as an industry leader in the AI revolution.<p>I recognize, however, that this is not a win for you. It is a bitter irony that we are using a narrative of &quot;technological replacement&quot; to mask a human-led planning error.<p>Support for Departing Team Members<p>Because this decision is a result of our planning—not your performance—we are providing the following transition package:<p>Salary: 20 weeks + 1 week per year of tenure.<p>Equity: Vested through the end of May.<p>Healthcare: 6 months of continued coverage.<p>Hardware: You may keep your corporate devices.<p>Stipend: $5,000 USD (or local equivalent) for your transition.<p>Closing Thoughts<p>To those leaving: I am deeply sorry. You built this company, and you remain world-class talent. Any organization would be lucky to have you, and I hate that the &quot;market-friendly&quot; version of this story implies otherwise.
 To those staying: We are going to &quot;own&quot; this AI-first identity moving forward. It is the core of how we will talk to our customers and our investors from this moment on. We will build with &quot;intelligence&quot; at the center, because that is the future the market has decided to reward.
