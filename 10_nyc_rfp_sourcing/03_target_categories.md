## "Pick 2" recommendation for capability statement

Going broad is a trap. The MWBE liaison reads the cap statement in 30 seconds and
files you under one category. **Pick 2 anchors** + 1 adjacency. Recommended pairing:

**Anchor A: Janitorial & sanitation consumables (#1)**
- Lowest cert burden, highest demand, fastest to first PO
- Customer hit list: DSNY, DOE Facilities, NYCHA, DCAS janitorial requirements contract

**Anchor B: Non-medical PPE (#2)**
- Higher margin once ANSI-certified
- Customer hit list: DSNY, Parks, DDC, DOT, NYCHA maintenance

**Adjacency: Hardware / industrial supplies (#4)**
- Natural cross-sell off PPE; same buyer
- Defensible against W.B. Mason / Grainger only on price+responsiveness

**Avoid in year 1**: textiles (UFLPA landmine), medical PPE (FDA), public-safety uniforms
(spec compliance wall), playground (liability).

## Sourcing intelligence — what to verify on the China side

For each anchor category, before quoting an agency, the user needs:

- [ ] **3 factory candidates** per SKU (price triangulation + redundancy)
- [ ] **MOQ tolerance** — agencies often start at 100–500 units; many factories want 1,000+
- [ ] **Sea freight + Section 301** built into landed cost (use FOB + 20–25% as quick check)
- [ ] **Certification documents the factory can supply**:
   - For PPE: ANSI cert from factory's lab + independent verification
   - For janitorial plastic: REACH / California Prop 65 if applicable
   - For uniforms: cotton traceability (XCMG / non-Xinjiang declaration)
- [ ] **Sample turnaround time** — agencies often ask for sample within 5–10 business days
- [ ] **Payment terms** — start at 30% T/T deposit + 70% against B/L copy; negotiate to 0/100 LC at scale

## Pricing the agency quote (rough heuristic)

```
Quote price = (Factory FOB × 1.25 [tariff] × 1.08 [freight+brokerage]) × 1.40 [your gross margin]
            = FOB × ~1.89

⇒ A $10 FOB SKU lands ≈ $13.5; quote agency at ~$19; expect to negotiate to $17–18.
```

Agencies typically benchmark against State OGS / GSA Advantage prices. Pull those before
quoting:
- [GSA Advantage](https://www.gsaadvantage.gov/) — federal but useful as a price ceiling
- [NYS OGS Centralized Contracts](https://ogs.ny.gov/procurement/centralized-contracts) — state contracts as benchmark
