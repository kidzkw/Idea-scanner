# DeepSeek Quarantine Rules

D1. DeepSeek-only numbers are never canonical.

D2. DeepSeek red-team objections may enter synthesis as questions, not facts.

D3. Any claim containing revenue, market cap, margin, CAGR, customer concentration, or date must have an external source URL and verbatim support.

D4. If DeepSeek contradicts 2 or more sources, synthesis must mark `requires_primary_adjudication`.

D5. DeepSeek can lower confidence, but cannot independently reverse a consensus.
